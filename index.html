Our Story

Founded in 2018, Chill Out Lingerie was born from a simple belief: every woman deserves to feel beautiful, comfortable, and confident in her intimate apparel.

We meticulously source the finest fabrics from around the world, working with artisan mills in France, Italy, and Switzerland to create pieces that are as luxurious as they are comfortable.

Our commitment to inclusive sizing and sustainable practices ensures that elegance is accessible to all, without compromising our planet.

Committed to Sustainability

We believe luxury and responsibility go hand in hand. Our packaging is 100% recyclable, we use ethically sourced materials, and partner with manufacturers who share our commitment to fair labor practices and environmental stewardship.
